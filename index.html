Welcome to your Journaly.

A note-taking app for you to journal your crypto trades.

For those who wonder why a trade journal is important, well a huge part of pyschology change in crypto is when you've spent time in the markets and realize you've roundtripped on account of not calculating yor impermanent loss and the cost of DCA-ing as well.

This is a mini app that allows myself and any others I share it with, document publicly with real time updates as well as cloud and local storage.
